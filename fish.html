Smoked Fish & Seafood

All our fish are brined and slowly smoked to 145° F. We use natural smoke from aged hickory.

Our bluefish and codfish are fresh Cape Cod wild catch, taken by local fisherman using environmentally sound and sustainable fishery practices. Our wild salmon comes fresh and unfrozen from Norway and offers beautiful texture and color. We use top quality sea scallops, harvested from New England waters.
