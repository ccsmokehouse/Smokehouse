Wholesale

Dave’s Cape Cod Smokehouse produces all natural hickory-smoked seafood products.  Local species of fish and seafood are fresh and wild caught from New England waters. All seafood products are smoked, processed, and packed at our fully licensed (retail, wholesale and catering) and HACCP certified commercial kitchen facility in Harwich, MA.

Our smoked fish is salt-water brined with all natural herbs and spices and hickory smoked. Our smoked fish fillets, scallops, pâtés, spreads and other fish products are made fresh every week.

Wholesale pricing is available to Specialty Food Stores, Restaurants, Schools, Institutions and Caterers as long as the minimum purchase requirements are met as stated below.

Hickory Smoked Seafood

All natural flavors, hardwood smoke.

  • Smoked Local Bluefish Fillet
  • Smoked Local Codfish Fillet
  • Smoked Atlantic Salmon Fillet
  • Smoked Local Sea Scallops
  • Smoked Seasonal Seafood

Retail Package: Vacuum-packaged portions, 8 oz. net. Sold by net pound.

Restaurant or Deli Bulk Pack: Whole fillets or whole scallops, vacuum-packaged or in fish boxes, at customer discretion. Sold by net pound.

Minimum order: 6 lbs.
