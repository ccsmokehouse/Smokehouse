Wholesale

Dave’s Cape Cod Smokehouse produces all natural hickory-smoked seafood products. Local species of fish and seafood are fresh and wild caught from New England waters. All seafood products are smoked, processed, and packed at our fully licensed (retail, wholesale and catering) and HACCP-certified commercial kitchen facility in Harwich, MA.

Our smoked fish is salt-water brined with all natural herbs and spices and hickory smoked. Our smoked fish fillets, scallops, pâtés, spreads and other smoked seafood products are made fresh every week.

Wholesale pricing is available to supermarkets, food service companies, specialty food stores, restaurants, schools, institutions and caterers as long as the minimum purchase requirements are met as stated below.

Ordering and Delivery Policy:

  • As a general rule, all product orders received at our offices before 12 noon on Monday will be processed and shipped later the same same week. Orders received after 12 noon on Monday will be processed and delivered the following week.
  • In some cases, orders received after 12 noon on Monday can still be processed and delivered later the same week, depending on size, product availability, et cetera.
  • Any surplus product (i.e. product in excess of actual orders) processed during our normal production runs will be made available on a first come – first serve basis. Note: Additional shipping/handling charges may apply.
